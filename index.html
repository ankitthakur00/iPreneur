The field of Social Entrepreneurship which has grown very rapidly in recent years leverages business management and entrepreneurial skills to pursue the multiple bottom lines of the social enterprise by keeping stakeholder value intact rather than only that of shareholder value. The Centre for Social Entrepreneurship came into existence after careful thought about the emerging needs of wider society to develop change leaders in the field of social sectors to create enterprises with a social purpose and thus generate wealth to enhance livelihood and sustainable development.

E-Cell, a student-led body under the Centre for Social Entrepreneurship, Tata Institute of Social Sciences is an initiative to bring awareness across India about the emerging field of Social Entrepreneurship. It aims to encourage young entrepreneurs to create sustainable social ventures that positively impact society. E-cell aims to help entrepreneurs to draw rich expertise through interactions, mentorship, and various entrepreneurial activities.

Through E-cell we support aspiring entrepreneurs by conducting workshops, expert talk sessions, mentorship programs, and competitions. We help them benefit from our networks and resources to encourage them to pursue entrepreneurship. Our flagship event of the year is iPreneur.
